Note: You only need espn_s2 and swid cookies if your league is not publicly viewable.

How to find your espn_s2 and swid:
Log in to your account on ESPN's fantasy basketball website. Right click anywhere on the website and click inspect option. From there click Storage on the top bar. Click the Cookies section for http://fantasy.espn.com. From there you should be able to find your swid and espn_s2 values. It remains the same through different sessions.

Example League:
league_id = 190002826
year = 2024
espn_s2 = blank
swid = blank
